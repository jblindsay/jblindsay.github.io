Getting Help

Whitebox GAT is distributed with extensive help documentation, which includes detailed descriptions of each of the plugin tools as well as several How To tutorials. Additional topical information about Whitebox GAT usage, features, and news can also be found at the Whitebox GAT blog. You may direct specific questions that you have to the community on the Whitebox Listserv.

Want to help with making Whitebox GAT even better?

Even if you don't feel comfortable with programming, you can help to strengthen the Whitebox GAT project by contributing to the help documentation. Contribute by editing and extending the existing documents. We're also always looking for you multi-lingual folks out there who can help with translating Whitebox GAT to other languages. Please contact John Lindsay (jlindsay@uoguelph.ca) for more information.
