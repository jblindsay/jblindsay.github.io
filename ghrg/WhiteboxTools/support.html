Support the WhiteboxTools Project

WhiteboxTools is an open-source project and is freely available. It does however take considerable resources to continue development of the software. Monetary contributions to help continue the development are also welcome and greatly appreciated. Please contact Prof. John Lindsay (jlindsay at uoguelph.ca) for details on making individual contributions. If your organization uses WhiteboxTools, there are a number of ways that you can lend support to the project. For example, your organization could issue a research grant or a contract to support ongoing development efforts. You may wish to provide a grant/contract to support the development of a specific analysis tool or new feature or improved documentation. Organizations may also provide funding to support graduate students at the University of Guelph to work on Whitebox related research. For more details, please contact Dr. John Lindsay, e-mail: jlindsay(at)uoguelph.ca.
