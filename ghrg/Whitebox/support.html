Support the Whitebox GAT Project

Whitebox GAT is an open-source project and is freely available. It does however take considerable resources to continue development of the software. Although we prefer contributions in the form of code, monetary contributions are also welcome and greatly appreciated. The Whitebox GAT project is currently unable to accept donations from individuals although we are working to set this up in the future. If your organization uses Whitebox GAT, there are a number of ways that you can lend support to the project. For example, your organization could issue a research grant or a contract to support ongoing development efforts. You may wish to provide a grant/contract to support the development of a specific analysis tool or new feature, improved documentation, or translation into a certain language. Organizations may also provide funding to support graduate students at the University of Guelph to work on Whitebox related research. For more details, please contact Dr. John Lindsay, e-mail: jlindsay(at)uoguelph.ca.
