About Us

Our story

Founded in 2020 and headquartered in beautiful Guelph, Canada, WG Inc. is passionate about providing innovative software solutions for data processing in the geospatial industry. The founders have over 25 years of combined experience in the field.

Our mission is to empower users with tools that simplify complex data tasks, making it easier to extract meaningful insights from their valuable lidar data.

We are committed to continuous improvement and customer satisfaction, ensuring our products evolve with the needs of our users.
